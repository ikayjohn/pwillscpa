About Us

WHO WE ARE

Your Trusted Accounting Partner

At Paul Wills & Associates, we are dedicated to providing world-class financial solutions tailored to the unique needs of businesses and individuals in Ontario and beyond.

Our team of experienced Chartered Professional Accountants brings decades of combined expertise to help you achieve your financial goals. We understand that every client is unique, and we take pride in delivering personalized service that addresses your specific needs.

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Paul Wills, CPA, CMA

Founder & Director

Paul Wills is the visionary founder and director of Paul Wills & Associates. With over 25 years of experience in public accounting and financial advisory services, Paul has established himself as a trusted advisor to businesses and individuals across Ontario and Nova Scotia.

As a Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA) and Certified Management Accountant (CMA), Paul brings comprehensive expertise in tax planning, business consulting, financial statement preparation, and strategic advisory services. His deep understanding of Canadian tax laws and regulations has helped countless clients optimize their financial strategies and achieve their business goals.

Paul founded Paul Wills & Associates with a commitment to providing personalized, high-quality accounting services that prioritize client success. Under his leadership, the firm has grown into a reputable practice known for its integrity, professionalism, and dedication to client satisfaction.
